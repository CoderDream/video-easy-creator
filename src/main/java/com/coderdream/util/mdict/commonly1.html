com·mon·ly

adverb
 OPAL W
/ˈkɒmənli/

  1. usually; very often; by most people通常；常常；大多数人地
    • Christopher is commonly known as Kit.克里斯托弗通常被称为基特。

    • avian influenza, commonly called bird flu禽流感，俗称禽流感

    • These fats are commonly found in processed foods.这些脂肪常见于加工食品。
