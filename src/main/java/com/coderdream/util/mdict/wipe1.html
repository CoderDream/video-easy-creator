Idioms Phrasal Verbs
  1.  
    to rub something against a surface, in order to remove dirt or liquid from it; to rub a surface with a cloth, etc. in order to clean it 擦；拭；抹；揩；蹭
    • wipe something (on something) Please wipe your feet on the mat.请在垫子上蹭一蹭脚。
    • He wiped his hands on a clean towel.他用一块干净的手巾擦了擦双手。
    • wipe something with something She was sniffing and wiping her eyes with a tissue.她边抽泣边用手巾纸拭擦眼泪。
    • wipe something + adj. He wiped his plate clean with a piece of bread.他用一块面包把碟子擦干净。
    Extra Examples更多例句
    • The plastic surface can be easily wiped.塑料表面容易擦拭。
    • When you've finished with it, simply wipe it clean with a damp cloth.你用完之后，就用一块湿布把它擦干净就行。

  2.  
    to remove dirt, liquid, etc. from something by using a cloth, your hand, etc. （用布、手等）擦干净，抹掉
    • wipe something (from/off something) He wiped the sweat from his forehead.他擦去额头上的汗。
    • wipe something away/off/up She wiped off her make-up.她把化的妆擦掉了。
    • Use that cloth to wipe up the mess.用那块布把脏东西擦掉。
    Extra Examples更多例句
    • She gently wiped the tears from her eyes.她轻轻地将眼角的泪拭去。
    • I wiped up the spilled wine.我把洒了的葡萄酒擦干净了。
    • He wiped the marks off the wall.他把墙上的标记擦掉了。
    • He paused to wipe the sweat from his forehead.他停下来擦了擦额头的汗水。

  3. to remove information, sound, images, etc. from a computer, video, etc.消除，抹去（计算机、录像带等上的信息） synonym erase
    • wipe something off (something) You must have wiped off that programme I recorded.你一定是把我录制的节目给抹掉了。
    • wipe something Somebody had wiped all the tapes.有人把所有磁带上录制的内容都抹掉了。
  4. to deliberately forget an experience because it was unpleasant or embarrassing抹去（旧事） synonym erase
    • wipe something from something I tried to wipe the whole episode from my mind.我设法把这整个经历从心中抹掉。
    • wipe something out You can never wipe out the past.你永远不能把过去一笔勾销。

  1. (informal非正式用语) to defeat somebody completely in an argument or a competition（在辩论或竞赛中）彻底打败对手，把对手打得一败涂地Topics Opinion and argumentc2
wipe the grin/smile off your face
  1. (especially in orders 尤用于命令) to stop smiling, especially because somebody else is annoyed and does not think the situation is funny敛起笑容（尤指因有人生气或觉得没什么好笑的）
    • Wipe that grin off your face and get back to work.别笑了，继续干活。
    • Wipe that stupid smile off your face.别那么傻笑啦。

wipe

noun
/waɪp/
/waɪp/
  1. an act of cleaning something using a cloth擦；揩；拭
    • Can you give the table a quick wipe?你把桌子快速擦一下行吗？
  2. a special piece of thin cloth or soft paper that has been treated with a liquid and that you use to clean away dirt and bacteria（湿）抹布，纸巾
    • Remember to take nappies and baby wipes.记住带尿布和婴儿湿巾。
    • antiseptic wipes无菌的擦布
    see also wet wipe
    3. Word Origin词源Old English wīpian, of Germanic origin; related to whip.古英语 wīpian，源自日耳曼语；与鞭子有关。
